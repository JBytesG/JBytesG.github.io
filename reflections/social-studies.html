Social Studies Reflection

My social studies classes at OWL have been somewhat limited but extremely fun. I have taken the 3 classes required to graduate, World History, Human Geo, and Gov, as well as electing to take APUSH over regular US History. Although I did not take any social studies electives I was still able to learn a lot about the aspects of social studies I am interested in.

I particularly enjoyed APUSH as a class. Its overall format of partly self directed learning of the content along with the more open class style lent itself very well to how I learn. The content was also particularly interesting to me. I struggle with finding motivation to work on subject matter that I do not find interesting but I never had that problem with APUSH. Especially the late 19th century and the early 20th include a lot of content I really enjoyed.

Social studies at OWL has been a good opportunity for me to make use of responsibility. Social studies summatives tend to be projects and not tests which means I need to do things on time. That is something I have trouble with so it takes a lot of work to keep up with it. I have had to take responsibility for my time management and work to make sure I can be successful. I have ended up mainly working on responsibility through social studies and it has really helped me.

I struggled with any time memorization was involved. I am almost unable to memorize specific facts on purpose. In general social studies classes could be taught with a focus on memorization which could be a huge problem for me but luckily OWL social studies avoid pure memorization for the most part. The broader perspective that my classes took really helped me with remembering facts by teaching them more in context. With context I can usually get to a fact if not pretty close so I was still able to do well. In APUSH there were a couple times that I never really got specifics into my head but those instances were sparse so the effects were mitigated.

I enjoyed my social studies classes at OWL. They taught me a lot about subjects I never would have thought to seek out but I ended up liking. At the same time I was able to refine my abilities to work on large projects and my time management. My classes in social studies have been an incredible experience.
